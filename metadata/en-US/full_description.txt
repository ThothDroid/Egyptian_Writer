This is a simple Android app for viewing, creating and exporting ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs. This app supports the professional writing of hieroglyphs using the Manuel de Codage.
Write ancient texts and browse all the avaible hieroglyphs on your tablet or phone.

Currently, the app supports four basic MdC code concepts
<ul>
<li><b>:</b> : To align signs vertically</li>
<li><b>*</b> : To align signs horizontally</li>
<li><b>()</b> : To group signs</li>
<li><b>!</b> and <b>!!</b> : To do a line break</li>
</ul>
